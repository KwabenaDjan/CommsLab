About Projects

PROJECT 1: Do you ever have the feeling that there is someone somewhere mostly, if not always, spying on you? Like there is someone watching your every step, knows whatever you are doing and follows you everywhere? Yes, that sense or irrational feeling is what we term “Paranoia”. My movie is titled “Paranoia”. In the movie, my aim was to highlight on this irrational fear and in the end get the viewer to understand that sometimes they can trust their instincts and they might always not be wrong about being paranoid.

PROJECT 2: Sharing is Caring: A Lunchbox Tale is an interactive comic hosted on the web. Through this story, we explore the narrative of Peter, our marble protagonist, on a normal day to school. The narrative unravels by him forgetting his lunchbox at home and the question is how will he have a meal this day. Through this project we were seeking to tell a love story through the expression Sharing is Caring. We wanted to achieve an interactive comic that the user can get involved through different buttons that allowed to reveal different aspects of the comic.

PROJECT 3 : As a project for Assignment 3 in the Communications Lab, my team members and I developed a website that includes visualisation, preferences for the website, and interactivity for audios. Our team explores the professional approaches to an audio website and increase its visualisation not only by lyrics but also through video. The “Melodic.verse” project emphasises on depression and how the transition to happiness can occur. However, instead of a song, a game or a video, we wanted to highlight the importance of the audio representation in this topic.

PROJECT 4: This project aims to develop an interactive website that hosts a video designed to challenge the moral decision-making skills of users while also providing them with information on proper laundry etiquette on campus. The interactive video follows the character of Aaron as he navigates through various scenarios in the laundry room. Users interact with the video by selecting what Aaron should do next in each scene, with the video then showing the consequences of the action selected by the user. The consequences range from minor mishaps to more serious incidents that highlight the importance of proper laundry etiquette.
